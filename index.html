About PromptPool

PromptPool is an innovative cryptocurrency token designed to incentivize and reward individuals who contribute high-quality prompts to build comprehensive databases for AI training. Our mission is to democratize AI development by creating a decentralized ecosystem where everyone can participate in shaping the future of artificial intelligence.

Why PromptPool?

AI models require vast amounts of diverse, high-quality training data to perform effectively. Traditional methods of data collection are expensive, time-consuming, and often lack the diversity needed for robust AI systems. PromptPool solves this by creating a community-driven approach where contributors are directly rewarded for their valuable input.

The PromptPool Process

1

Connect Your Wallet

Connect your cryptocurrency wallet to the PromptPool platform using WalletConnect or other supported wallet providers.

2

Submit Quality Prompts

Create and submit high-quality prompts that can be used to train AI models. Focus on clarity, creativity, and diversity.

3

Community Validation

Your prompts are reviewed by the community and AI systems to ensure quality and relevance for training purposes.

4

Earn Rewards

Receive PromptPool tokens directly to your wallet based on the quality and usefulness of your contributions.

Reward Structure

Contribution Tiers

Bronze Contributors: 1-10 PPOOL tokens per approved prompt

Silver Contributors: 5-25 PPOOL tokens per approved prompt + bonus multipliers

Gold Contributors: 10-50 PPOOL tokens per approved prompt + premium bonuses

Platinum Contributors: 25-100 PPOOL tokens per approved prompt + exclusive benefits

Quality Factors

Rewards are determined by prompt originality, clarity, training value, community votes, and AI assessment scores. Higher quality contributions earn significantly more tokens.
